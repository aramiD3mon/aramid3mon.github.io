About Oak & Ember

We believe quality care begins with genuine compassion and professional expertise.

Oak & Ember, LLC is a licensed assisted home care facility nestled in Maricopa County, Arizona. We provide a safe, nurturing and home-like environment where each resident receives dignity, warmth and personalized attention.

Our model is guided by a Registered Nurse and supported by a two compasionate caregiving team, ensuring proressional oversight and attentive daily care.

What We Offer

Our Care Services

Personalized support tailored to each resident's unique neeeds - delivered with warmth, professionalism, and genuine compassion.

24/7 Professional Care

Round-the-clock supervision and support from our trained caregiving team, with Registered Nurse oversight to ensure the highest standard of care.

  • Vital sign monitoring
  • Emergency response
  • Night-time supervision
  • Health assessments

Medication Management

Our RN-led team ensures accurate, timely medication administration and coordination with physicians and pharmacies.

  • Prescription coordination
  • Dosage tracking
  • Physician communication
  • Medication reviews

Daily Living Support

Compassionate assistance with everyday activities to help residents maintain independence, dignity and quality of life.

  • Bathing & grooming
  • Meal preparation
  • Mobility assistance
  • Housekeeping

Social & Emotional Wellness

Enriching activities and meaningful social engagement to support mental health and a deeper sense of belonging.

  • Group activities
  • Family visits support
  • Recreational programs
  • Spiritual care

Nutritious Meal Planning

Home-cooked, balanced meals tailored to each resident's dietary needs and preferences, prepared with care every day.

  • Dietary accommodations
  • Hydration monitoring
  • Special diets
  • Snacks & beverages

Transportation & Appointments

Coordinated transportation to medical appointments and therapy sessions.

  • Doctor appointments
  • Therapy sessions
  • Pharmacy & Walmart Wednesday runs

About

A calm, elegant home with thoughtful presence.

Oak & Ember is thoughtfully designed as a warm, refined home for older adults who thrive in an intimate setting with attentive, day-to-day support. Our residence is led by a Registered Nurse with decades of experience, supported by a dedicated team of caregivers, all focused on truly personalized care.

Care

Personalized support for daily living.

Our home is planned for approximately 10 residents with 2 caregivers, allowing for a more personal approach to assistance, routines and companionship - far more attentive than larger facilities.

Timeline

Opening path

The facility will be ready in the first quarter of 2027. Family inquiries and early sign-ups begin November 2026. We’re building slowly but razor focused on our timeline and vision - ensuring all of the puzzle pieces fit perfectly.

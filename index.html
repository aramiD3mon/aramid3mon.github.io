About

A calm home with a thoughtful presence.

Oak & Ember is thoughtfully designed as a warm, refined home for older adults who thrive in an intimate setting with attentive, day-to-day support. Our residence is led by a Trauma ICU Registered Nurse with decades of experience, supported by a dedicated team of caregivers, all focused on truly personalized care.

Care

Personalized support for daily living.

The home is planned for approximately 10 residents with 2 caregivers, allowing for a more personal approach to assistance, routines and companionship.

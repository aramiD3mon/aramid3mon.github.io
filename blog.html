Signs It Might Be Time for Assisted Living.

  • Difficulty with daily activities: bathing, dressing, grooming, or preparing meals consistently.
  • Medication errors or forgetfulness: missing doses or confusion about timing.
  • Unexplained weight loss or poor nutrition: skipping meals or relying on convenience foods.
  • Frequent falls or mobility issues: trouble with stairs, balance, or getting around safely.
  • Changes in personal hygiene or home maintenance: unkempt appearance or neglected household tasks.
  • Increasing isolation or loneliness: withdrawing from friends, family or hobbies.
  • Worsening chronic conditions: harder to manage diabetes, heart issues or arthritis at home.
  • Safety concerns: leaving doors unlocked, stove on or wandering.
